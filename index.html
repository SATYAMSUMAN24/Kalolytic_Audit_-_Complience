Why Komplieye?

We Help Banks & NBFCs Stay Audit-Ready & Compliant

Komplieye is an integrated Audit Management System (AMS) and Compliance Management System (CMS) built specifically for banks and NBFCs to stay ahead of regulatory requirements and internal risks. It combines audit automation and compliance workflow management into one comprehensive platform.

Designed specifically for the banking sector, Komplieye comes pre-configured with industry best practices and RBI guidelines out-of-the-box.

By unifying audit and compliance in a single solution, Komplieye eliminates silos and manual effort – empowering your team to save time, improve accuracy, and stay "audit-ready" at all times.

To streamline audits, ensure RBI compliance, and reduce operational risk for financial institutions through intelligent automation.

We empower banks and NBFCs to ensure 100% compliance, mitigate risks proactively, and respond to regulatory changes with agility.

To be the leading unified audit and compliance platform that delivers measurable outcomes: time saved in audits, zero missed filings, and a stronger compliance culture.

Creating a future where regulatory compliance is automated, transparent, and risk-free for all financial institutions.
