About Komplieye

Transforming Audit & Compliance for Financial Institutions

Komplieye is an integrated Audit Management System (AMS) and Compliance Management System (CMS) built specifically for banks and NBFCs. We combine audit automation and compliance workflow management into one comprehensive platform designed to keep financial institutions ahead of regulatory requirements.
